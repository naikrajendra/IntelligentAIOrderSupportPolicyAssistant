# Returns and Refunds Policy

## Eligibility
- Standard return window is 30 calendar days from the delivery date.
- Items must be unused and returned with original packaging.
- Personalized, perishable, and final-sale items are not eligible for return unless defective.

## Refund Rules
- Refund method defaults to original payment method.
- Shipping fees are refundable only when the return reason is seller fault (wrong, damaged, or defective item).
- For customer-remorse returns, outbound shipping is non-refundable and return shipping may be deducted.

## Processing Timelines
- Return authorization (RMA) should be issued within 24 hours of request validation.
- Refund is initiated within 3 business days after warehouse quality check passes.
- Bank posting times may add 2 to 7 business days after initiation.

## Escalation Conditions
- Escalate if refund is pending more than 10 business days after return received.
- Escalate if tracking confirms return delivered but no warehouse scan within 72 hours.
- Escalate high-value orders (order total greater than 500 USD) to supervisor approval.
