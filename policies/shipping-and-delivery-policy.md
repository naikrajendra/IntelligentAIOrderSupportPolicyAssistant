# Shipping and Delivery Policy

## Shipment Stages
- PLACED: Order accepted and awaiting fulfillment.
- PACKED: Order packed and waiting for carrier pickup.
- IN_TRANSIT: Carrier has possession and tracking is active.
- OUT_FOR_DELIVERY: Local courier attempts delivery.
- DELIVERED: Carrier confirms delivery completion.

## Delivery Commitments
- Standard shipping target is 3 to 7 business days after dispatch.
- Express shipping target is 1 to 2 business days after dispatch.
- Delivery estimates may extend during severe weather or regional disruptions.

## Lost and Delayed Orders
- Mark order as delayed if expected delivery date is exceeded by more than 48 hours.
- Open carrier investigation when no tracking scan appears for 72 hours.
- Mark order as potentially lost when no movement occurs for 7 calendar days.

## Support Actions
- For delayed orders, offer proactive updates and revised ETA.
- For potentially lost orders, offer replacement or full refund based on customer preference.
- For failed delivery attempts, verify address and arrange re-delivery or hold-for-pickup.
